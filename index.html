What is Manchester United's best lineup?

Read Time: 2 Minutes

Author: Radfas Akorli

In recent times, the international break has often been the best period in the year for Man United fans - solely because there are no United games to ruin your weekend; and oftentimes, ruin the the following week as well. Instead, we usually spend 2 weeks reminiscing about all the great (and not so great) players we used to have, that gave us memorable moments - since most of the current crop haven't been providing these for us! However, today, we will use the 3 games we've played so far to deduce the best lineup we have at our disposal, accounting for: balance, the coach's style, "form" and fitness. Before we select players, we must first decide on a formation...

Now this is like a riddle that even the coaches themselves can't seem to solve - luckily I'm here to reveal the answers! In my opinion, the only issue, when it comes to the formation (we have MANY issues elsewhere) is the midfield. Do we go with a 2-man pivot or just one sitter (a '6'? Do we play with a designated attacking midfielder or do we have 2 central midfielders ('8s') "sharing" that responsibilty? I personally would opt for a 6 with twin 8s either side; just to close up the gaps we constantly see in the midfield. Even though you can solve this with better coaching, it's clear that ETH isn't meeting the needed standards to this formation would be beneficial as a result. Now this has been established, the lineup will be a 4-3-3 with a 6 and two 8s.
